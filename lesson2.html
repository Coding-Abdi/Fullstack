Recipe

  1. Finely dice 1 medium onion and 3 cloves of garlic
  2. Chop up 1 bell pepper
  3. Season seabass with salt and pepper
  4. Pour 2 Tbsps of sunflower oil into heated pan
  5. Saute onions and garlic for a few minutes
  6. Pour 2 Tbsps of sunflower oil into a separate heated pan and place seabass in that pan
  7. After a few minutes transfer the seabass from its own pan and into the pan with the garlic and onions
  8. Add chopped bell pepper into that pan
  9. Mix well together for a few minutes and serve
